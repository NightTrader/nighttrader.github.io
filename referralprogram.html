What is the NightTrader Exchange referral system?

The NightTrader Exchange referral system is a 5-tier program that rewards users for bringing new traders to the platform. As you progress through the tiers, you earn a percentage of the trade fees generated by your referrals and their subsequent referrals.

How do I join the referral program?

To join the referral program, simply sign up for a NightTrader Exchange account and start referring new users with your unique invite code.

How do I become a CryptoKnight?

You become a CryptoKnight by directly referring someone to NightTrader Exchange using your invite code.

Do I earn rewards if my referrals don't trade?

No, you only earn rewards when your referrals actively trade on the platform.

How do I progress to higher tiers?

To progress to higher tiers, your referrals must also refer new users. For example, to become a CryptoBaron, your direct referrals must have their own referrals.

Can I earn from multiple tiers simultaneously?

Yes, you can earn from multiple tiers simultaneously as long as you have referrals at different levels.

How are the earnings calculated for higher tiers?

Higher tiers earn a percentage of the remaining trade fees after lower tiers have been paid out. For example, CryptoBarons earn 24% of the fees remaining after CryptoKnights have been paid.

Is there a limit to how many referrals I can have?

There is no limit to the number of referrals you can have at any tier.

How can I maximize my earnings in the referral program?

To maximize your earnings: Invite active traders Encourage your referrals to refer others Aim to climb to higher tiers Stay engaged with your referral network

How often are referral rewards paid out?

First every week after we do a maintaince usually on sunday. Later with more users it will be more frequently.

Can I track my referral earnings?

Yes, you should be able to track your referral earnings through your NightTrader Exchange account dashboard. For detailed information, please check the platform or contact support.
